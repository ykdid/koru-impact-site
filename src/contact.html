Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with Koru Impact

What makes Koru Impact different from other consulting firms?

Our unique four-pillar ecosystem combines rigorous social science research with practical interventions, all grounded in deep cultural understanding of the Turkish business context. We don't just consult—we partner with you to create sustainable transformation.

Do you work with international organizations?

Yes, while our expertise is particularly strong in the Turkish cultural context, we work with organizations globally. Our evidence-based approach and systemic empathy principles are universally applicable.

What is the typical timeline for a project?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Initial assessments typically take 2-4 weeks, while comprehensive transformation programs can span 6-18 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

How do you measure success?

We use evidence-based metrics tailored to your organization's goals, including employee well-being indicators, performance metrics, and sustainability impact measures. All our interventions include clear measurement frameworks and regular progress reviews.
